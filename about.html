About RevHaus

We help brands grow with clarity and consistency.

RevHaus is a South African-founded digital marketing and growth agency that brings global expertise to local and international brands. We exist to help businesses scale smarter through data-driven marketing, strategic storytelling, and technology that turns awareness into measurable growth.

Our approach blends creativity with precision. We do not chase trends. We build systems that generate demand, create meaningful connections, and deliver consistent results.

The Founder

Keaton Ghall is a growth strategist with more than a decade of experience working with companies across the UK, Europe, and the US. After years of helping agencies and enterprises optimize their digital presence and sales pipelines, Keaton saw a gap in how many South African businesses approached digital marketing. Too often it was reactive, disconnected, or built on outdated playbooks.

He started RevHaus with a simple conviction:

“If we can help local and global brands speak with clarity, strategy, and confidence in the digital space, we can help them grow sustainably.”

RevHaus was born out of that vision to merge strategy and execution under one roof and to empower businesses to thrive in an evolving digital economy.
